HEALTHY EGO – Verein für Kunst- & Kulturförderung e.V.

We are actively coordinating the collaboration between artists. The HEALTHY EGO association is connecting musicians, photographers, producers, software developers, fashion designers, graphic designers, videographers and many other creatives who are supporting each other.

In addition, we offer artists a platform: by realising events, our exciting variety of projects can be experienced. In addition to organising exhibitions, concerts and readings, we also support our association members in the conception and promotion of publications.

We want to support young people who are recognising their potential: Are you an artist or creative? Do you want to network and receive support in realising your ideas? We are always on the lookout for talent to make our association even more diverse! We are location-independent - anyone can participate from anywhere.
We are looking forward to hearing from you!
