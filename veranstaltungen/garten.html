Our second exhibition entitled "HEALTHY EGO Garten" took place in July 2018. It highlighted the versatility of our young association: contemporary art - including drawings by Lisa Kull and Paul Schlenzig - was combined with digital light installations, while our artists Bloody9ine and $uijin live produced new tracks. We are especially proud of the venue, which is located in one of the most significant landscape gardens in Germany: The exhibition room, built in 1775, is part of the pleasure ground of the Elector Friedrich Carl von Erthal, who had the former electoral game preserve redesigned into a park.
